♟️The Chess rules♟️

There are rules in chess that you have to follow to make the game functional. Here are a list of chess rules:

Castling
♘ Castling is used to move your king out of the center from attacks. You can do castling for different tactics. In order to castle, you must meet all the requirements: You have not moved the king or rook once. There are no pieces between the rook and king. The king is not in check. A piece is not attacking squares where the king will castle and will land. You can castle in either direction.
Pawn Promotion
♘ When a pawn reaches the the opposite row of the board, it can turn into a knight, bishop, rook or a queen but it cannot promote into itself.
En Passant
♘ This move is unique and can only be used by pawns. In order to do the en passant move, you need to meet all the requirements: The opposite pawn is three ranks(rows) away from the rank from its starting position, the opposite pawn is on the file(coloumn) beside the capturing pawn, you can only capture the pawn right after when the pawn moves.
Check
♘ Check is when the king is being attacked by another piece. When the king is in check, you must move the king out of check. You can also use other pieces to block the check if you want.
50 Move Rule
♘ The 50 move rule is when both players make 50 moves without capturing pieces or moving pawns
