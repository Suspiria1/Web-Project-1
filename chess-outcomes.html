♟️Chess Outcomes♟️

In chess, games can result in a checkmate, draw or a stalemate. When you win a game, your opponent's king has no legal moves and is in check(checkmate). A draw is when both players cannot make any moves that can win the game. A stalemate is when you have no legal moves and your king is not in check.
