What is Chess?

Chess is a game that involves strategy and critical thinking. It is one of the oldest board games in the world. Billions Millions of people play this game for fun or for competition.

How to Play Chess

Chess is a two player game. The goal is to capture the enemy king. There are rules that you have to follow in order to play chess properly. Before you play chess, you will need to setup a chessboard. Each player takes turns moving chess pieces and each move has a chess notation. The person with the white pieces always starts first. The players continue to make chess moves until they reach an outcome. When you become stronger at the game, your rating increases.
